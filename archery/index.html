About our School:

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen. From Beginner to Elite, archers of all ages and ability learn about this exciting Olympic and international sport.

Practice Ranges

Our facilities include both indoor and outdoor ranges to fit your desired training environment.

Indoor Facility

  • Fourteen 10-yard lanes
  • Fourteen 20-yard lanes
  • Two private coaching rooms

Outdoor Facility

  • Twenty six 20-yard lanes

Hours

Monday-Saturday: 10:00 A.M. - 8:00 P.M.

Sunday: 10:00 A.M. - 6:00 P.M.

Our Offerings

Straight to the Point archery offers both group and private lessons for beginning to advanced students. Our classes are taught by certified instructors who guide our students in a range of archery styles with safety in mind.

We also offer event schedluing for:

  • Youth Group Events
  • Corporate Gatherings
  • Birthdays
  • Tournaments
