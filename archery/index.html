Our Offerings

Straight to the Point archery offers both group and private lessons for beginning to advanced students. Our classes are taught by levels 1 and 2 USAA certified instructor who guide our students in a range of archery styles with safety in mind.

We also offer event schedluing for:

  • Youth Group Events
  • Corporate Gatherings
  • Birthdays
  • Tournaments

About our School

Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen. From Beginner to Elite, archers of all ages and ability learn about this exciting Olympic and international sport.
